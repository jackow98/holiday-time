About Holiday Time

Your personal travel companion since 2024

🎯 Our Mission

At Holiday Time, we believe everyone deserves the perfect vacation. Our intelligent quiz system analyzes your preferences - from climate choices to budget constraints, from adventure levels to accessibility needs - to recommend destinations that truly match what you're looking for. We cater especially to travelers with special requirements, ensuring everyone can explore the world comfortably.

✨ What Makes Us Special

  • ★ Personalized Recommendations: Our quiz matches your exact preferences to destinations worldwide
  • ★ Accessibility Focus: Special options for travelers with mobility, dietary, or other requirements
  • ★ Real Information: Actual prices, real hotels, and verified airline details
  • ★ Global Coverage: All 195 countries with detailed information for each
  • ★ Budget Options: From backpacker-friendly to luxury, we cover all ranges
